Clip It


An automatic Twitch highlight generator

What is Clip It?

Clip It allows you to find the highlights of all your favorite streams. Clip It was built by Joheen Chakraborty and Vincent Huang to ensure that even the busiest college student could still watch their favorite esports match, streamer, or tournament. We find all the highlights of a VOD and give you the timestamps for each highlight, so all you need to do is click on the timestamp to watch the most thrilling plays.

We are both avid esports fans and with Clip It, we can now watch our the newest esports matches in a matter of minutes.

How to use

Getting started with Clip It is easy. Follow the steps below to view your highlights in no time!

  1. Find the twitch VOD you would like to download.

    Note: the clip url should be in the format https://twitch.tv/videos/502873475

  2. Enter the link to the twitch VOD into the box above and click the "Get Highlights" button
  3. Finally you can click on any of the given timestamps to view the greatest plays of the VOD.
How it works

We use Artificial Intelligence to find the greatest clips and plays of your given VOD. We use a whopping combination of Natural Language Processing, Sentiment analysis, and Twitch Chat Frequency to ensure that the clips we give you are truly the best parts the match.
